What do the axes labels mean?

Valence: A measure of an emotion's positive/negative polarity.
In other words, a scale of the pleasant/unpleasantness of an emotion. It can be thought of as the felt difference between, say, happiness/contentment, and unhappiness/sorrow.

Emotions with lower valence values are perceived to be adjacent to sadness, anger, gloominess, anxiety, melancholia and depression, whereas emotions with higher valence values are perceived to be adjacent to happiness, relaxation, elation, excitement, contentment and calmness.


Arousal: A measure of an emotion's energy, or lack thereof.
It can be thought of as the felt difference between, say, sadness/unhappiness, and anger/rage. Though both feelings are considered two sides of the same emotional coin, the perceived difference between them is thought to be related to their difference in energy, or arousal.

Emotions with lower arousal values are perceived to be adjacent to relaxation, calmness, sleepiness, lethargy, gloominess and sorrow, whereas emotions with higher arousal values are perceived to be adjacent to excitement, elation, alertness, tension, nervousness, anger and rage.


How do I use this tool?

Open Spotify Web Player, login with your details and open Chrome Developer Tools. You can do this by pressing F12 on Windows, or by pressing cmd + option + I on MacOS. Find a tab that says 'Network' , and under it will be a burst of requests that happens on your logging in. Once this subsides, open a request with name 'events' , and scroll until you see 'Authorisation' .
Copy the chunk of text after 'Bearer', this is your access token.

Paste into the field at the top of this page, press submit and it should take you to another webpage with a Valence-Arousal plane like the ones seen on the right of this page. Think about the sort of mood you want for your playlist, and click a point on the plane you feel corresponds to it.

A playlist will then be generated with the 10 emotionally closest songs in your Spotify listening history to the point you chose. Once your playlist has been generated, you should be redirected its Spotify page, where you can decide what to do with it.

Happy playlisting! :)
